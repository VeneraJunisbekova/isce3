Detailed Description

Data structure to represent ECEF state vectors of imaging platform.

There are two representations of the orbit information that are currently carried by this data structure. A vector of isce::core::StateVector for a serializable representation and vectors of time-stamps, positions and velocities to speed up computations. All Time stamps are assumed to be UTC. All positions are in meters and in ECEF coordinates w.r.t WGS84 ellipsoid All velocities are in meters/sec and in ECEF coordinates w.r.t WGS84 Ellipsoid
