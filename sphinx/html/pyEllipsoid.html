pyEllipsoid¶

pyEllipsoid is meant to capture common geometry functions that are performed on an ellipsoid.

class isceextension.pyEllipsoid(a=6378137., e2=.0066943799901)¶

Python wrapper for isce::core::Ellipsoid

Parameters

  • a (Optional[float]) – Semi-major axis of Ellipsoid in m.

  • e2 (Optional[float]) – Eccentricity-squared.
